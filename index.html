Design Strategy

Pricing Transparency

  • Show full cost breakdown before cart — no surprise fees at checkout
  • Real-time price comparison across restaurants with "Best Value" badge

Reliable Customization

  • Verified modification system with restaurant-confirmed SLAs
  • Persistent dietary profiles auto-applied to every order

Delivery Visibility

  • Live GPS tracking with real-time driver location and countdown ETA
  • Proactive delay alerts with transparent reasoning and revised time
Designed to eliminate uncertainty at every touchpoint of the ordering journey.
